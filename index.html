I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Michigan State University. I received my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. I.V. Ramakrishnan.

My research area includes Human-Computer Interaction, Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence, and Accessible Computing. I am interested in designing and building AI-powered intelligent user interfaces to support better human-computer interaction.
